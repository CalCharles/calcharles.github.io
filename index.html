Personal Information

I am a PhD student at the University of Texas at Austin studying Computer Science. I am working in the Personal Autonomous Robotics Lab (PeARL) with Professor Scott Niekum. I am interested in better understanding how robots can complement humans. I am developing hierarchical object-centric methods to improve robotic manipulation.
